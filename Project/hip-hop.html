History

Hip Hop came from New York City in the 1970s. With the help of music, Hip Hop became a way for people to express their feelings about the turmoil going on during that time. That is why some people can see Hip Hop being a negative thing. Like any other form of dance, Hip Hop also has its different styles to it. There is break dance, whacking, tutting, and others.

Benefits

You wouldn't believe the strength needed to help with Hip Hop dancing. Hip Hop helps to strengthen not only muscles but the bones too. This style of dance also helps with motor skills. Hip Hop can also reduce stres, help with teamwork skills, and is a great way to express emotion.
